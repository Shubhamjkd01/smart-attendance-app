Key Features

Everything you need for modern attendance management

📱

QR Code Attendance

Quick and contactless attendance marking using time-limited QR codes. Teachers generate codes, students scan to mark presence instantly.

📅

Smart Timetable

Automated schedule management with free period detection. View daily classes and identify productive time slots.

🎯

Personalized Activities

Get activity suggestions based on your interests and career goals. Make the most of free periods with targeted learning.

📊

Real-time Tracking

Monitor attendance and progress in real-time. Access detailed reports and analytics instantly.

🔒

Secure & Reliable

Time-limited QR codes ensure security. All data is stored safely with proper authentication.

NEP 2020 Aligned

Supports personalized and experiential learning as per National Education Policy 2020 recommendations.

How It Works

For Teachers

  1. Login to your teacher account
  2. View your assigned classes
  3. Generate QR code for attendance
  4. Display QR code to students
  5. View real-time attendance reports

For Students

  1. Login to your student account
  2. View your daily schedule
  3. Enter QR code when displayed
  4. Get personalized activity suggestions
  5. Track your attendance history
